About The Team

WE LIVE FOR THE ICE

Cones Belgrade is more than just a hockey team — it's a community of passionate athletes who believe that the spirit of the game transcends age and professional status. Founded in Belgrade, we've built a culture of excellence, brotherhood, and relentless improvement.

Whether you're a seasoned skater or just lacing up for the first time, Cones Belgrade welcomes everyone who bleeds blue and orange. We compete hard, train harder, and celebrate together.
